Our Services

Hydro Jetting for Sewer & Storm Drains

When traditional cleaning methods fail, hydro jetting gets the job done. Our high-powered equipment clears blockages, including grease buildup and debris, up to 350 feet through multiple manholes. We provide thorough cleaning to keep your system flowing.

Ideal For:

  • Apartment complexes
  • Commercial buildings
  • Storm drains and culverts

Root Removal

Roots in sewer lines can cause severe blockages and damage. Using advanced tools like the Warthog WT Classic, we safely and effectively remove roots from clay and other sewer lines, restoring flow and preventing recurring problems.

Sewer Backup & Emergency Clearing

Sewer backups are no match for us. Whether it’s grease, roots, or a collapsed line, we quickly identify the issue and clear your system. If the problem involves city infrastructure, we provide detailed reports to help get repairs moving.

Past Projects Include:

  • Clearing 1,800 feet of backed-up sewer line for a warehouse and plaza
  • Resolving severe backups at apartment buildings

Storm Drain & Culvert Cleaning

Heavy rain can overwhelm storm drains and culverts, leading to backups. We specialize in clearing these systems of grease, debris, and other obstructions to ensure they function properly when it matters most.

Video Camera Sewer Inspections

Not sure what’s causing the issue? Our video camera inspections provide a clear view of the problem, allowing us to locate blockages or damage with precision. This ensures fast, effective repairs with minimal disruption.
